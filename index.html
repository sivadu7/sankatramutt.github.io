About the Mutt

The Thiruvotriyur Sankara Mutt is a sacred spiritual institution associated with the Kanchi Kamakoti Peetam. It stands as a living centre of Advaita Vedanta, Vedic learning, and traditional worship.

Spiritual Importance

Established in connection with the lineage of Adi Shankaracharya, the Mutt conducts daily poojas, Vedic chanting, and spiritual observances in accordance with age-old customs.
