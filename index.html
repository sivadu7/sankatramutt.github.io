About the Mutt

The Kanchi Kamakoti Sankara Mutt at Thiruvotriyur is a revered Hindu spiritual institution associated with the sacred Kanchi Kamakoti Peetam. Rooted in the timeless philosophy of Advaita Vedanta, the Mutt stands as a centre of devotion, learning, and spiritual peace.

Situated near the ancient Thyagaraja Temple and Vadivudai Amman Temple, the Mutt forms an integral part of Thiruvotriyur‚Äôs rich spiritual heritage.

Spiritual Heritage

Thiruvotriyur is one of the oldest spiritual centres of Tamil Nadu. The Sankara Mutt here preserves Vedic traditions, promotes spiritual discipline, and guides devotees on the path of Dharma, Bhakti, and Jnana.
