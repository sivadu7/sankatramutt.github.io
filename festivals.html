Major Festivals

Thiruvotriyur Sankara Mutt celebrates important Hindu festivals with devotion, traditional rituals, and spiritual fervour.

  • Shankara Jayanthi – Birth anniversary of Jagadguru Adi Shankaracharya
  • Guru Purnima – Honouring the Guru Parampara
  • Navaratri – Worship of the Divine Mother with special poojas
  • Maha Shivaratri – Night-long prayers and devotion to Lord Shiva

Monthly & Special Observances

  • Ekadashi
  • Pradosham
  • Amavasya & Pournami days
  • Special Abhishekams on auspicious occasions

Spiritual Atmosphere

During festival days, the Mutt resonates with Vedic chants, devotional hymns, and collective prayers, offering devotees a deeply spiritual experience.
